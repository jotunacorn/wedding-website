We're getting married

The date is set to the 13th of August and we would love to see you there!

Schedule

Ceremony on the beach 14:00 - 15:00

There will be a ceremony on the beach of Ängelsbäck, with some speeches and performances. If the weather is to bad/windy, we might change the location to a more suitable one nearby. We won't back down from a little rain so come well prepared.

Photography and mingle15:00 - 16:00

After the ceremony there will be time to take some photos with us, and get to know the other guests.

Bygdegårds party 17:00

Afthe the ceremony and photographs we'll head on to Västra Karups Bygdegård. At the bygdegård we'll serve a buffet and have dinner together. Vegitarian/Vegan options will be available, but please note in your RSVP if you have any other dietary restrictions.

Dancefloor 22:00

After the delicious dinner, we'll redress the venue to a dance floor.

Midnight snack 24:00

When people start to wear out, we'll serve some snacks and then continue the party with some more dancing!

Brunch 10:00

If you're not in a hurry there will be brunch at Ängelsbäck during the sunday morning. The couple will also open their presents during this occation.

Dresscode

Ceremony 14:00

Clothes to be outdoors
There's no demand to have your finest clothes, since the ceremony is outdoors. Of course we love if you're dressed nicely, but in reality bad weather might occur in Sweden in August. Hopefully you can have the same clothes as during the dinner, but otherwise your best raincoat will do.

Wedding dinner 17:00

Formal/Summer festive
To the dinner we would like it if you wear formal clothes, or something summer festive.

Speeches and performances

If you like to perform something during the dinner (like a speech, dance, juggling etc.) then we would like for you to sign up to toastmaster@ja2022.se. Please mention roughly what you're going to do, and an approximate duration.

Closest train station

The closest station is Förslövs station (10 minutes by car from the ceremony), but more trains stop at Båstad and Ängelholm (30 minutes by car from the ceremony).

Parking - Ceremony

We would like it if people try to fill their cars with as many persons as possible, since the number of parking spaces for the ceremony are limited. For the party after the ceremony there's plenty of space

In the area around the cermony theres usually many cars parked if it is a sunny summer day, but the larger parking lots (fitting roughly 20 cars each) are located at Vistorps hamn, Segeltorpsvägen and Ängelsbäcksvägen/Välmossavägen

If you arrive in a camper, there's more info in the section accommodations.

Accommodations

At the bjäre peninsula theres several places to stay, we would like it if the guests can arrange a place to sleep by themselves. If this is hard, or if you got any other questions (like if you're traveling by yourself and would like someone to share a room with) feel free to contact Oscar Nyh at +46 70 868 44 92, oscar@nyh.se.
